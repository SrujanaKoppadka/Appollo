Our Story

Zambia's Kombucha
Pioneers Since 2019

Appollo Products Ltd was born from a passion for natural wellness and a belief that Zambians deserve access to world-class health drinks crafted right at home. In 2019, we introduced kombucha to the local market — a naturally carbonated herbal drink steeped in thousands of years of brewing tradition.

What started as a vision quickly grew into a beloved brand. From the very first bottle to the shelves of your favourite store, every step of our journey has been guided by three principles: purity, quality, and care. We use only the finest natural ingredients — no shortcuts, no compromises.

Today, we proudly offer two signature kombucha varieties: our crisp Raw Kombucha and bold Ginger Kombucha — each brewed with a live SCOBY culture and packed with live probiotic bacteria to support your wellbeing every single day.

Our Journey

How It All Began

From a bold idea to Zambia's leading kombucha brand — a journey built on passion and purpose.

2019

The Beginning

Appollo Products Ltd is founded in Zambia with a mission to bring authentic, naturally brewed kombucha to local communities.

2019 – 2020

First Batches

Our signature Raw Kombucha — a crisp, green-tea-based brew — hits the market. Zambians discover the taste of natural fermentation for the first time.

2021

Ginger Kombucha Launches

Responding to customer love for bold flavors, we introduce Ginger Kombucha — a fiery, warming brew that instantly becomes a bestseller.

2022 – 2023

Growing Across Zambia

Distribution expands across Lusaka and beyond. Appollo kombucha becomes a staple health drink in homes, gyms, and health stores nationwide.

2024 – Present

Zambia's Favourite

Appollo Products Ltd is recognized as Zambia's #1 kombucha brand, continuing to brew with the same love, care, and natural ingredients from day one.

What We Brew

Two Remarkable Kombuchas

Each variety is crafted with care — naturally fermented, packed with probiotics, and bursting with distinct character.

Signature Blend

Raw Kombucha

"Clean, crisp, and alive."

Our Raw Kombucha is the pure expression of the ancient art of fermentation. Brewed from premium green tea and a living SCOBY culture, this light, refreshing drink delivers a gentle natural fizz and a wealth of beneficial probiotics in every sip.

It's clean on the palate, lightly tangy, and subtly sweet — the perfect everyday health companion. Add it to your morning routine or enjoy it as a mid-day refresher.

Ginger Kombucha

"Bold, warming, and alive."

For those who crave a little more fire in their wellness routine, our Ginger Kombucha delivers. The warming heat of natural ginger root is expertly balanced with the tangy depth of our live fermentation culture — creating a drink that's both invigorating and nourishing.

A beloved bestseller, it's the go-to choice for anyone who wants their daily health drink to pack a bold, memorable punch.
