Step by Step

How Every Bottle Is Made

From raw ingredients to the bottle in your hand — every step is carried out with expertise, hygiene, and an uncompromising commitment to quality.

🌿
Step 01

Sourcing the Best Ingredients

It all starts with quality. We carefully source premium black and green tea leaves, natural cane sugar, and fresh dry ginger root — selecting only ingredients that meet our strict standards for purity and potency.

Premium black tea · Pure green tea · Natural sugar · Fresh dry ginger
1
2
🍵
Step 02

Brewing the Tea

The selected tea is carefully steeped at precise temperatures to fully release its rich flavors, natural antioxidants, and beneficial compounds. This forms the nutrient-rich liquid base that will carry the fermentation.

Precise steeping temperature · Full flavor extraction · Antioxidant-rich base
🧬
Step 03

Fermentation with Live SCOBY

The brewed tea is introduced to our living SCOBY — a Symbiotic Culture of Bacteria and Yeast — along with a starter liquid from a previous batch. This activates natural fermentation, transforming the tea into a probiotic-rich, lightly acidic kombucha over several days.

Live SCOBY · Natural fermentation · Probiotic development · Controlled environment
3
4
🫚
Step 04

Flavoring — The Ginger Touch

For our Ginger Kombucha, real dry ginger root is infused at this stage, giving it the signature bold warmth and anti-inflammatory punch our customers love. Our Raw Kombucha is left in its pure, clean form — letting the green tea and natural fermentation flavors shine through.

Real dry ginger infusion (Ginger) · Pure fermentation flavors (Raw)
🔬
Step 05

Quality Checks

Every single batch is rigorously tested before it goes anywhere near a bottle. We check for pH balance, probiotic activity, taste consistency, and overall safety — ensuring every bottle of Appollo kombucha lives up to our promise of quality.

pH testing · Probiotic verification · Taste assessment · Safety checks
5
6
🍶
Step 06

Bottling & Packaging

The kombucha is hygienically filled into 330ml recyclable plastic bottles in a clean, controlled environment. Each bottle is sealed to lock in the natural carbonation and living probiotic cultures, preserving freshness all the way to your hands.

Hygienic fill · 330ml recyclable bottles · Airtight seal · Natural carbonation preserved
✅
Step 07

Ready to Enjoy

From our facility to your fridge — Appollo Kombucha is ready to delight. Refreshing, naturally carbonated, alive with probiotics, and authentically Zambian. Keep it chilled and enjoy one bottle a day for the best health benefits.

Refrigerate & enjoy · One bottle daily · Raw Kombucha or Ginger Kombucha
